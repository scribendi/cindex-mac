Rearranging text
 

Cindex provides shortcuts to move text around between and within fields.

Flipping Fields

To exchange (flip) the contents of any two fields, place the insertion point anywhere in the upper field and hit CONTROL +. To pull up a heading to replace the one at the level above (half flip) place the insertion point anywhere in the upper field and hit CONTROL –. Cindex also provides toolbar buttons for executing full- and half-flips.

Cindex provides a powerful 'smart' flip, that automatically exchanges the capitalization of headings, and moves prepositions and articles (or, in the case of See also references placed in the locator field, the prefix phrase) when it flips the fields.

To enable smart flipping, choose Preferences… (Edit menu) and on the Editing tab check Smart Flip. To specify the articles and prepositions that can be moved during a flip, choose Smart Flip Words… from the Document menu.

When you use a keyboard shortcut to make a full flip (CONTROL +) or a half flip (CONTROL –) you can override the default mode (smart or not) by holding down SHIFT while you give the command.

Acronyms

If a record field contains a phrase followed by text in parentheses (for example, an acronym or its expansion) you can exchange the contents of the leading text with the text in parentheses. Click anywhere in the field then click the toolbar button or hit CONTROL P.

Cindex moves text smartly: if the leading phrase begins with a word from the Smart Flip set, that word is left untouched at the beginning of the field.
